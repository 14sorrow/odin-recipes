Basted Eggs

This recipe produces a delicious, simple egg with satisfying, set whites and a creamy, runny yolk. It's similar to a poached egg, but more flavorful and less fussy.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a skillet with melted butter.
  2. When the whites are mostly set, add water and cover.
  3. Cook until some of white has set over the yolk.
